Help & Guide

Features

  • 📚 Album Management: Add, edit, and delete albums with artist and year information
  • 🎵 Song Tracking: Add unlimited songs to each album with ratings and comments
  • ⭐ 5-Star Ratings: Rate each song individually to track your favorites
  • 🔍 Filter & Sort: Find albums by artist, year, or name
  • 💾 Auto-Save: Your data is automatically saved to your device

Tips

  • Use meaningful album titles for easy searching
  • Leave comments to remember your thoughts about songs
  • Sort by rating to quickly find your favorite albums
  • Your data is stored locally on your device

Getting Started 🚀

  1. Visit the My Albums page to start your collection
  2. Create albums and provide a comment
  3. Add songs to each album with 5-star ratings
  4. Leave comments on songs
  5. Sort and filter your collection by various criteria
